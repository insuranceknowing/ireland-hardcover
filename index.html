Description

The Emerald Isle is brought to life in Spectacular Ireland through words and breathtaking photos, a beautiful set of 140 color photographs by some of Ireland’s best landscape photographers and an elegant text by Peter Harbison, one of the most respected voices and renowned archaeologists in Ireland. This beautiful volume celebrates Ireland, a small island nation of exceptional beauty, fascinating history, and evocative myths, where both old and new flourish. We take a scenic tour capturing the soul of this beautiful island from ancient standing stones and stunning rural scenes to vibrant cities with architectural sophistication.

 

Ireland’s brief history places the pictures in historical context: from ancient Celtic dolmens to abbeys and castles, from green pastures, heather fields, and seaside cliffs to formal gardens, and from ancient villages to contemporary Dublin’s rush. Yet there is a lot more to Ireland than the breathtaking countryside. Most of the country’s magic resides inside the people themselves; these are people who love their soil. It is a rich historical, mystical and mysterious heritage that makes Ireland a favorite destination for visitors and armchair travelers alike.
